Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling:
    • Whisk together sugar, flour, cornstarch and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl.
    • Bring to a boil and continue cooking until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue:
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually.
    • Spread over pie.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.

This is our favorite recipe here at Granny's Pies. It has been enjoyed by pie fans for many years. We hope you'll find that this recipe is delicious and easy to follow; it's a piece of cake pie!
